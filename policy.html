Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Learn how we protect and handle your data.

Effective Date: January 15, 2025 | Last Updated: January 15, 2025

Our Privacy Promise

Vertex: Dotgrid respects your privacy. We collect minimal information, store most data locally on your device, and never sell your personal information to third parties.

üîç Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Player Name: Optional display name for multiplayer games (stored locally on your device)
  • Game Settings: Preferences such as sound, haptic feedback, and visual settings (stored locally)
  • Support Communications: When you contact us for support, we keep records of our correspondence

Automatically Collected Information

  • Game Progress: Local game statistics, scores, and achievements (stored locally on your device)
  • Usage Analytics: Basic app usage patterns through Google Analytics for Firebase (anonymized)
  • Crash Reports: Automatic crash reporting to help improve app stability (no personal data)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility

üíæ How We Store Your Data

Local Storage (Most Data)

  • iOS: Data stored using iOS Keychain, UserDefaults, and local Hive database
  • Android: Data stored using Android SharedPreferences, internal storage, and local Hive database
  • Network Multiplayer: Direct device-to-device communication over local WiFi - no external servers involved
  • Your Control: You can delete all local data by uninstalling the app

Cloud Services (Limited)

  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data sent to Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Crash Reports: Automated crash logs sent to help improve app stability
  • No Personal Data: These services do not receive any personally identifiable information

üîó Third-Party Services

Google Analytics for Firebase

  • Purpose: Understand how players use the app to improve the experience
  • Data Collected: App usage patterns, session duration, feature usage (anonymized)
  • Opt-Out: iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security; Android: Google Settings > Usage & Diagnostics

Google AdMob (Free Version Only)

  • Purpose: Display advertisements in the free version of the app
  • Data: Device advertising ID, general location (country/region), age group
  • Family-Friendly: All ads are set to family-friendly content only
  • Remove Ads: Purchase Pro version to completely remove all advertisements

In-App Purchases

  • iOS: Processed through Apple App Store (subject to Apple's privacy policy)
  • Android: Processed through Google Play Store (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Our Access: We only receive confirmation of successful purchases, no payment information

üì± Platform-Specific Privacy

iOS Privacy Features

  • App Tracking Transparency: We request permission before any tracking for advertising purposes
  • Camera Access: Only used for QR code scanning in network multiplayer (with your permission)
  • Local Network Access: Only used for local WiFi multiplayer games
  • Privacy Controls: Manage ad tracking in Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising

Android Privacy Features

  • Required Permissions: INTERNET (analytics/ads), VIBRATE (haptic feedback), CAMERA (QR scanning), ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE (multiplayer)
  • Privacy Dashboard: View and control all app permissions in Android settings
  • Advertising Controls: Manage ad personalization in Google Settings > Ads
