🙋 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Pro purchase?
iOS: Go to the main menu → Settings → Restore Purchases. Android: Main menu → Pro version → Restore. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.
The game is running slowly or crashing
Try these solutions in order: 1) Close and restart the app. 2) Restart your device. 3) For older devices, try using smaller board sizes (3×3 instead of 7×7). 4) On Android, clear the app cache in Settings → Apps → Vertex → Storage → Clear Cache.
How do I play online with friends?
Use "Network Game" from the main menu. One player hosts and shares a QR code, others scan it to join. Both devices must be on the same WiFi network. Camera permission is required for QR scanning.
What's included with Vertex Pro?
Pro version removes all advertisements and provides unlimited gameplay. You also get priority support and early access to new features. Future updates will include premium themes and advanced AI opponents.
Are AI difficulty levels available?
Currently, the AI plays at a single balanced difficulty level that's suitable for most players. Multiple difficulty options (Easy, Medium, Hard) will be added in a future update based on player feedback.
Haptic feedback not working
iOS: Check Settings → Sounds & Haptics → System Haptics is enabled. Android: Check that vibration is enabled in your device settings and that the haptic feedback toggle is on in the game settings. Some older devices may have limited vibration support.
How do I get a refund?
iOS: Contact Apple Support or visit reportaproblem.apple.com within 90 days of purchase. Android: Visit Google Play Store → Menu → Account → Order history → Find the app → Request refund (within 48 hours) or contact Google Play support.
Can I transfer my progress to a new device?
Currently, game progress is stored locally on each device and doesn't transfer between devices. This design choice prioritizes your privacy by keeping your data local. Cloud save functionality may be added in a future update.
The app won't launch or keeps crashing
Try these steps: 1) Force close the app completely and reopen. 2) Restart your device. 3) Check if you have the latest app version in the App Store/Play Store. 4) Free up device storage (need at least 1GB free). 5) If the problem persists, contact support with your device model and iOS/Android version.

💡 Tips & Strategy

🎯 Gameplay Strategy

  • Think Ahead: Avoid giving your opponent easy boxes to complete
  • Chain Reactions: Try to set up multiple boxes you can complete in one turn
  • Sacrifice Strategy: Sometimes giving away a few boxes early prevents bigger losses later
  • Control the Center: Central lines give you more strategic options

📱 Performance Tips

  • Smooth Gameplay: Close other apps before playing for best performance
  • Battery Life: Lower screen brightness to extend play time
  • Network Play: Stay close to WiFi router for stable connections
  • Storage: Keep at least 1GB free space on your device

🌐 Multiplayer Tips

  • QR Scanning: Ensure good lighting when scanning QR codes
  • WiFi Connection: Both devices must be on the same network
  • Stable Connection: Stay within 30 feet of the WiFi router
  • Backup Plan: Note the host's IP address for manual connection
